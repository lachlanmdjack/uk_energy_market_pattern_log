# Market Log — 26 June 2026

--

## Observations and Drivers

**Primary observation:**
> My prediction yesterday was that based on wind generation forecast, prices would not tank towards £0 on Friday, and a possibility of high peak prices. The lowest midday period was £65, but 2nd cheapest was £104. Peak prices did not rocket again, but steadily rose to £165. As of 8:30pm, prices have steadily increased to nearly £200, likely fueled by French interconnectors having plummeted from nearly 4GW import to 0.5GW export. 
> 
> French nuclear reactors are generating some of the lowest generation amounts, 50% of peak capacity, meaning UK interconnectors are more susceptible to flipping from import to export, dramatically increasing UK prices.
