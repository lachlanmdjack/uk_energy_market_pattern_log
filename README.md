# UK Energy Market Pattern Log

An eye into the daily UK energy market, explaining day-after observations.

Each week day there will be commentary on some of, but not exclusive to, the UK energy market in these forms: 
> SSP Prices (System price/ imbalance price)
> 
> N2EX Day Ahead Pricing
> 
> Long term gas prices
>
> Forward curve block patterns and intraday arbitrage opportunities
