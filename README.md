# UK Energy Market Pattern Log

An eye into the daily UK energy market, explaining day-after observations.

Each week day there will be commentary on some of, but not exclusive to, the UK energy market in these fundamental forms: 

**Power**
-
> **Causes:** 
> 
> Power stack, renewable energy generation and gas turbines
> 
> Interconnector behaviour
> 
> Gas prices and spark spreads
> 
> Demand
>
> **Metrics:**
> 
> SSP Prices (System price/ imbalance price)
> 
> N2EX Day Ahead Pricing
>

**Gas**
-
> Long and short term sentiment
>
> Planned and unplanned disruptions to supply 
> 
> Henry Hub and LNG transportation logistics

Forward curve block patterns and intraday arbitrage opportunities
