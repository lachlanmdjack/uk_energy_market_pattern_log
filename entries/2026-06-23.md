## Observations and Drivers

**Primary observation:**
> Over the last 2 days of heat in the UK, the post-sunset demand from (baseload-shaped) airconditioning in europe has flipped interconnectors at night (as Germany/Spain are relatively more dependent on solar output), causing SSP in the UK to jump from £90 in SP37 to £173 and £80 in SP32 to £172 the next half hour (Monday had thunderstorms to solar output dropped much earlier than usual for the day after a summer solstice). 
> 
> Looking at the claude-coded 'Net_Interconnectors' graphing from these two days, the UK goes from importing +7.7GW from interconnectors to +1.5GW on Sunday, and +6.6GW to exporting -2GW overnight (3pm starts the ramp up in exporting).
